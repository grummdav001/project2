Rest

Rest recharges your body and restores your energy again and a well rested body can improve your mood and preformance mentally and physically

Night Sleep

  • Have a sleep schedule
    • Organize your bed time to allow you to get 8 hours of sleep and be up early
      • Set an alarm for the morning
  • Set up your room for a perfect sleep
    • Make your bed
    • Cover the window and turn off the lights
  • Sleep correctly and comfortably
    • Wear (or don't wear) what helps you sleep better
    • Keep a straight posture like a soldier while you sleep

Taking Rest Breaks When Needed

  • After completing a very engaging exercize or non-stop work at school or a job, it is important to take mental and physical breaks
    • Doing this helps ripped muscle heal faster and can improve preformance compared to not taking breaks when needed
